How To Apply

Register to portal if you haven't registered yet.

Registration procedure:

1.Click on SignUp button.

2.Fill up all the details. After submitting details a mail will be sent to your account for confirmation.

3.Confirm by clicking the link sent to you in previous step.

4.Fill up all the personal details and submit the form.

Allocation procedure:

1.Login to your acount.

2.Check availability and set your preferences.

3.If room is available, it will be allocated else request will be sent to the current owner of the room.

4.If owner accepts your request, it will be allocated else try for some other room.

If no preferences are set then room will be allocated randomly.
